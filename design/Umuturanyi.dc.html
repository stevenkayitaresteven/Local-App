Design brief · system & assumptions
Umuturanyi
umuturanyi · “neighbor” in Kinyarwanda

A neighborhood marketplace & community app for Rwanda — buy and sell nearby, find local businesses, save together, and look out for one another. Warm and playful, but practical and trustworthy.
